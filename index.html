Research Experience

01
2023–2025 · Master's Thesis Chapter 3

Gated Fine-Tuning Dynamic Feature Self-Aggregation Network for Low-Dose CT Denoising

Proposed a dynamic feature self-aggregation attention-guided network that combines multi-scale attention mechanisms with refined residual feature learning for effective noise artifact identification and suppression. Introduced a Gated Fine-Tuning differentiable gating unit for dynamic modulation and nonlinear fusion of residual features. Validated on clinical datasets with superior PSNR/SSIM performance against existing methods.

CNN Attention Mechanism Residual Learning Gating Mechanism PyTorch
02
2024–2025 · Master's Thesis Chapter 4

Transform-Domain Fast Fourier Deformable Enhancement Network for Low-Dose CT Denoising

Designed a frequency-domain denoising network combining spatial and transform domain processing. Employed Fast Fourier Convolution (FFC) for multi-scale feature extraction and integrated deformable convolution modules for adaptive feature enhancement. Evaluated on the Mayo 2016 LDCT benchmark, achieving significant improvements in noise removal, texture preservation, and edge retention.

FFT / Frequency Domain Deformable Convolution Multi-scale Feature Extraction Mayo LDCT Dataset
